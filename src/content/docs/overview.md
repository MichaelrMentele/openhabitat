Many people, when they think of longevity think of starvation or deprivation, but in fact, those who are serious do the opposite. A system that is on the edge won't survive the first shock it runs into. Longevity is having extreme resilience, and extreme resilience requires excess so that when something bad happens you can shrug it off. They don't look ahead to the future, they are trapped in the present making local choice after local choice until they hit a dead end and die.
