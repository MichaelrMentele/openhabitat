A perfect exposome for everyone, designed once, released openly, free for anyone to copy.

What is the **exposome**? It's the sum total of everything that has ever touched you since the moment of conception. 

While we cannot turn your family, or the school you attend into "code", what we can do is turn your *habitat* into code. By doing so we can distribute a blueprint for optimal habitats to everyone and solve this key piece of the puzzle.

OpenHabitat is that blueprint. Every system, every space, every product decision worked out in the open so optimal environments evolve as fast as the threats do.
