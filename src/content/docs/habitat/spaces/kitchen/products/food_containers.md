## What We Were Looking For

Clean storage for leftovers from batch prepping.

## Key Directions Explored

You can get all stainless steel like [this](https://lifewithoutplastic.com/products/stainless-steel-rectangular-food-storage-container-with-seal-1600-ml-54-oz).

## What We Selected

Rubbermaid Brilliance and similar lines pair tempered or borosilicate glass containers with Tritan or polypropylene (PP #5) plastic lids and silicone gaskets. Cold storage has near-zero risk with the lid on.

## Why

It was at the local target and we needed a same day replacement!
