## The Problem

If you are to optimize a single room in your house the bedroom is the one that matters most. 

Why? Because sleep is where all the gains are made:
- Learning: You do not actually learn during the day, you learn at night
- Recovery: Your muscles and tissues don't grow during the day, they grow when you rest

Poor sleep is equivalent to poor performance in everything and thus a poorer life. 

The problem is that, by default, modern bedrooms as well as all the goods in them are not designed for optimal sleep. First, they have no way to measure sleep performance, but then they also have bad light, bad temperature, bad air, and weak sound proofing.

## The Goal

Our goal is perfect sleep. 

Now we need to focus on the furnishings in the room to make sure they are optimal.
