The habitat is everything around you that touches your body without permission: the air you breathe, the water you drink, the light hitting your retina, the chemicals offgassing from your furniture. Most of it is invisible. All of it adds up.

Two layers:

- **[Systems](/habitat/systems/air/)** — automating ambient cross-cutting concerns: air, water, light, sound, radiation, materials, foods, textiles, circadian.
- **[Spaces](/habitat/spaces/bedroom/)** — optimizing the rooms where you live: bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, office, laundry, entries, car, yard.

Work through Systems first (set-and-forget infrastructure) before optimizing individual Spaces.
