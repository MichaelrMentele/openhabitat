## When Purchasing Food

Scan the barcode or look it up with Yuka, if it has any yellow or red ingredients, do not buy it. If it scans red due to salt or calories, you can ignore since it may be fine in your overall diet but we draw the line at any ingredients that are not green themselves.

## When Purchasing General Products

Scan or look it up with ThinkDirty. If it is not green, do not buy it.

## When Purchasing Kitchenware

Do not buy non stick. They are all poisonous and will scratch and leach. 

Buy glass, wood, ceramic, stainless, or cast iron. That's it.

## When Purchasing Clothes or Textiles

Generally, if it is synthetic do not buy it.

No matter what always look for one of:
- MADE SAFE certification
- OKEO-TEX + GOTS certifications

You can google search for reputable brands with these certifications.
