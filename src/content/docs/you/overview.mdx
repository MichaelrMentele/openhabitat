Four sections:
- **Compass**: Measure where you are and where you're going
- **Behaviors**: behaviors not attached to any particular space
- **Rhythms**: behaviors that run on a schedule
- **Body**: Conceptual pages on each system — physiology, threats, measurement

The absolute minimum to get started is:
- Get an initial Compass reading to see where you are at
- Add the Annual Rhythm to check in

From there I recommend setting your habitat up for automatic health before coming back to the other rhythms.
