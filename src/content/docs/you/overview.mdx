Four sections:
- **[Compass](/you/youth/)**: Measure where you are and where you're going
- **[Behaviors](/you/behaviors/purchasing/)**: behaviors not attached to any particular space
- **[Rhythms](/you/rhythms/dailies/)**: behaviors that run on a schedule
- **[Body](/you/body/gut/)**: how the body works

## Get Started

Ideally start by:
- Get an initial [Compass](/you/youth/) reading to see where you are at
- Add the [Annual Rhythm](/you/rhythms/yearlies/) to check in

Then you will see that the systems actually work. If you prefer to just take it on faith you can also jump straight into setting your [habitat](/habitat/intro/) up for automatic health.

Then measure once you are ready to optimize.

I'm a skeptic, so personally, I prefer to have before and after measures. It also makes a great case study to [contribute](/give/).
