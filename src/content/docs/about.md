## How to Use

This manual is laid out in order of highest *automatic* return on effort — starting with top-level framing in Body, set-it-and-forget-it benefits in Systems and Spaces, and ending with personal Rhythms and Behaviors. Work through Body → Systems → Spaces → Rhythms → Behaviors in that order.

- [Body](/you/body/) — what matters, how to measure, and cross-cutting principles
- [Systems](/habitat/systems/air/) — automating ambient cross-cutting concerns like air and water
- [Spaces](/habitat/spaces/bedroom/) — automate optimal places you do things
- [Rhythms](/you/rhythms/dailies/) — when to do what in your spaces
- [Behaviors](/you/behaviors/breakfast/) — specific, composable actions you take

Within every topic we use the same structure:

- **Problem** — the status quo
- **Goal** — the ideal future
- **Gap** — the delta
- **Solution** — the physics and measures
- **System** — the implementation
- **Deploy** — how to set up and maintain it
- **Proof** — evidence the system works
