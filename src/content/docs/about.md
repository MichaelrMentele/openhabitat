## How to Use

This manual is laid out in the order of most benefit, starting from the top level framing in Body, followed by set it and forget it benefits in Systems & Spaces and finally the Rhythms and Programs that requires you to change your behaviors. Work through Body, then Systems, and then Spaces in the order they are given for maximum gain vs. effort.

- Body -- how it works, what matters, how to measure, and cross cutting operating principles
- Systems -- automating ambient cross cutting concerns like air and water
- Spaces -- optimizing the places so you can do things better
- Rhythms -- when to do what in your spaces
- Programs -- specific, composable, behaviors you do

Within every section of this manual we structure it like:
- The Problem (the status quo)
- The Goal (the ideal future)
- The Gap (the delta)
- The Solution (the physics and measures)
- The System (the implementation)
- The Proof (the system works)
